What is visitor management?
Visitor management involves monitoring and controlling access to a location by visitors, contractors, and non-employees. Automated systems streamline this process by enhancing security and simplifying registration and check-in. Cloud-based solutions often include screening questions, digital sign-in forms, and visitor badges for a secure and efficient experience.

How a visitor management solution can help you?
Put simply, a visitor management solution helps your organization keep track of anyone visiting your physical workplace. It’s an integral part of your workplace visitor policy. But a good visitor management solution is so much more than that. A visitor management system can help you:

Make your workplace work better for your people
It doesn’t matter how great you think your visitor experience is. Your visitors have to think so, too. To know this, you have to ask them. In addition to sending a thank you email after their visit, include a short survey about their experience. This lets visitors know you actually care how they feel, and gives you objective insight into what others think about your team’s hospitality. In addition to qualitative survey feedback, your VMS should provide you with robust analytics.
